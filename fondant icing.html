FONDANT ICING

Ingredient:

  • Icing Sugar – 1 kg
  • Gelatine – 1tbsp
  • Glucose – 4 tbsp |1⁄2 cup
  • Glycerine – 1 tbsp
  • Shortening (optional) – 1 tbsp
  • C.M.C – 1 tbsp
  • Water - 1⁄4 cup

Procedure

  1. Mix in icing sugar and c.m.c, set aside.
  2. Dissolve gelatin in 1⁄4 cup of water
  3. Place it in a bowl on top of a boiling water. Wait till it melts.
  4. Then pour glucose into it.
  5. Dissolve it together
  6. Add shortening and glycerine
  7. Pour in the mixture of icing and c.m.c, knead the mixture together.
  8. It may be a bit hard just keep placing your palm in water and then mix till a gum- like but firm paste is formed. Make sure there are no lumps
